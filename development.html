Project Evolution

The journey of PowerTitan evolved as I was going.

Key Milestones

  • In September I created the entire frontend layout, and keyed out the important things I will have in my website
  • In October I worked tirelessly to create the Backend component. The Backend component consists of Supabase for authentication, Render as my API port, and Node.js+express for server-side functionality.
  • In Novemeber and December I added new features like a nutritionial tracker, and an AI chatbot to help guide you on this journey!

Mentor's Role

My mentor helped support me throughout this journey. My biggest issue during this time was Backend, and she gave me some tips on where to start. Without my mentor, I would not be where I am today!
